Managing multiple OpenAI Codex accounts is painful. Hit rate limit on account A, manually switch to B, lose track of who has quota left. With a team of 8 sharing 3 Codex Pro subscriptions, it gets worse fast.

GoClaw v2.34.0 introduces Codex OAuth Pools — combine multiple accounts into one unified pool with automatic request routing. Three strategies: round_robin, priority_order, or primary_first with failover.

The nice part: agents just set override_mode: "inherit" and pick up pool config from the provider. Change pool membership once at provider level, every agent updates automatically. Real-time quota monitoring per account via OpenAI Usage API.

Result: 8 users, 1 agent, 3 accounts pooled. Requests distribute evenly, auto-failover on quota exhaustion. Zero manual intervention.
